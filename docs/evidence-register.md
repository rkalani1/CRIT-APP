## Review contract

Before a quantitative clinical claim is promoted to “checked,” record the exact population, intervention, comparator, endpoint, horizon, analysis set, raw event counts when available, and primary source. Recompute the arithmetic independently and date the review. Label claims that have not completed this process **needs confirmation**.
