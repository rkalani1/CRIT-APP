## What this is

A **Web Edition** teaching book for neurologists, stroke clinicians, and
epidemiologically minded trainees who need practical, decision-focused critical
appraisal—prediction vs causation, absolute effects, bias structure, trial and
observational design, diagnostics, prognosis, and evidence-to-practice.

## What this is not

- Not a digitized commercial EBM handbook  
- Not a paste of paper abstracts, guideline instrument tables, or publisher PDFs  
- Not institutional UW/HMC policy  

All teaching figures on this site are **original** (code-drawn for this curriculum).
Methods standards (CONSORT, TRIPOD, GRADE, etc.) are referenced **by name** with
links to their home organizations—not reproduced as copyrighted instruments.
